EDITABLE LINKS
Open index.html and find:

const LINKS = {
  googleForm: "YOUR_GOOGLE_FORM_LINK",
  weddingAlbum: "YOUR_WEDDING_ALBUM_LINK"
};

googleForm:
Use the public Google Form link. The website button and the separate
wedding-hall QR code should both open this same form.

weddingAlbum:
After the wedding, replace the placeholder with your Google Photos
or Google Drive album link.

QR STRATEGY
- Chocolate invitation QR: opens the wedding website homepage.
- Wedding hall QR: opens the Google Form directly.
- Both the website button and hall QR lead to the same upload form.
